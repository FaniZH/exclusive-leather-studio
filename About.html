Our Story

About Exclusive Leather Studio

Exclusive Leather Studio is a premium furniture destination located in Pinetown, Durban, specializing in luxury leather furniture designed to elevate modern living spaces. Our collections combine elegant design, superior craftsmanship, and long-lasting comfort.

We offer a curated selection of leather sofas, corner lounge suites, recliners, dining furniture, coffee tables, and bedroom pieces carefully chosen to bring sophistication and warmth into every home.

At Exclusive Leather Studio, we believe furniture should not only look beautiful but also provide exceptional comfort and durability. Each piece reflects timeless style, high-quality materials, and attention to detail that transforms ordinary rooms into luxurious living spaces.

Our Founder

The Story Behind Exclusive Leather Studio

Exclusive Leather Studio was founded by ZH MADLALA, a passionate furniture designer who believed that furniture should combine comfort, elegance, and durability.

Growing up around furniture workshops, ZH developed a deep appreciation for craftsmanship and design. Over the years he refined his skills and gained industry experience working with leather materials and luxury interior pieces.

With years of experience and a deep respect for quality materials, he created Exclusive Leather Studio to craft furniture that is both visually stunning and built to last.

ZH motivation comes from his belief that a home should reflect elegance and personality, and furniture is at the heart of that expression. Today every collection reflects his commitment to quality, style, and timeless design.
